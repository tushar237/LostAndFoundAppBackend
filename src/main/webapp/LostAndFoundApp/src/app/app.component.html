About Us

What we do and how to get started.

We provide a global platform for the people who have lost or found something want to spread the word.

We provide you the place to post details about the item and you so that someone can contact you if they find an item matching your details can return it to you.

Lost or found item can be of any type, it can be a Person,vehicle,mobile,electronics,Document,identity, luggage etc. So if you have lost something or recieved something make a post. And we hope the best for you. :)
